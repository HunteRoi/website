Proof of concept

The proof of the pudding is in the eating. We put our open source tools for data integration to the test during open Summer of code. 10 students have developed 3 concrete projects based on the suggestions from the open call for ideas!

Check out the three selected projects

01. Hopper

Providing tailor made commute paths for (future) e-bike owners to stimulate the shift towards a more sustainable mobility.

02. Configuroute

A tool for making personalized configuration files for route planning.

03. Horison

Helping small groups of people find suitable places to meet up.
